The Bay Area's European Bodyshop Specialist

Bodystyle is the Bay Area's premier auto body shop for all your European and other fine automotive car needs. In business since 1976, we pride ourselves on providing the highest quality to our customers. Specializing in Porsche, BMW, and Mercedes, along with other European makes, we are a full service auto body center, using only factory-manufactured parts and paints. Bodystyle offers a comprehensive package of services, from small jobs to complete restorations. If you need an insurance quote, we will be glad to help. We will also work with your insurance company to ensure that only factory-manufactured parts are used. We look forward to working with you.
